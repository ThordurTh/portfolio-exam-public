About

Welcome to my portfolio website!
My name is Thordur Frimann and am a multi-media design student at KEA. In my portfolio can a variety of assignments be found that I worked on from August to December 2021. They include websites, design documentation and UX solutions.

In the process have I learned how to code the structure of a website with HTML, how to apply colours and fonts, position items and make animations with CSS and make basic functions with Javascript. I have also acquired knowledge on programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Xd.

But there is more to making a website than knowing how to use programs and programming languages. Learning the fundamentals of how to design with UX in mind has been very valuable.
