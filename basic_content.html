Basic Content

This theme had its focus on plan, shoot and edit a video for a client’s website and making changes to the client’s website in relation to colours, typography, logo and overall style in cohesion with the video presented.

To achieve that was it essential to learn how to take notes from the client’s debrief and address problems, know how to plan and shoot a video using different techniques and editing in Adobe Premier or an equivalent program. The final product should provide the client with a more beneficial website.

A Story Without Words

Before we had a debrief with the client, were we taught how to make a short video. The requirements was that it told a story without words in a minute and showed that we planned, shot and edited accordingly. I grouped up with a classmate to make a video about a missing rabbit. We made a storyboard and planned every shot and its location. It was a great practise for the main assignment to come.

Treatment

We formed groups of 5 after the client’s debrief and began comparing our notes. The client runs the “Free Fridge” project where anyone can donate and/or take food from a public fridge. The client mentioned a few problems that they had been dealing with such as, struggling to get volunteers and spreading the word. We wanted to tackle those by making a video that shows how easy it is to help others in need.

It starts out by having 4 people opening a fridge at the same time. 3 of them in their homes, take something out while the last one at the free fridge, closes it empty handed. A few moments later can those 3 people be seen walking towards the free fridge with vegetables in their hands to the 4th person’s joy.

Storyboard

We made a storyboard which shows each shot, its location, camera transtition, and description. We originally had 15 shots in mind but it got reduced to 13 in the editing process. It became really helpful to use it as a guide while shooting to make sure nothing was forgotten.

Customer Pitch

We finished the video, implemented it on a website and started building our pitch to the client. It covered our general process of the project from the debrief to the final outcome, how we did it and how we thought it would benefit the client.
